Hole in the Wall

An easy recycling method.

Features

Sustainable

The threshold for recycling is decreased and life is made easier for both the customer and the manufacturer.

Time-saving

Recycled materials are automatically sorted, reducing the need for human intervention and saving time in the long run.

Refunds

Customers can redeem the value of their recyclables for use at Walmart stores.
